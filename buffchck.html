Best Ever Buffalo Chicken Dip

Yield: 6
Author: Shelby Hayes

Best Ever Buffalo Chicken Dip

Prep time: 10 MinCook time: 30 MinsTotal time: 40 Mins
This buffalo chicken dip recipe is quick and easy! Perfect for any potluck, tailgate, or Saturday snack! It takes 10 minutes to prep, bakes for 30* minutes, and it's that easy!

Ingredients

Instructions

  1. Mix shredded chicken and hot sauce in a large bowl.
  2. Stir in your Ranch dressing.
  3. Add garlic powder and Mix.
  4. Pour in the cheese and Mix
  5. Next, stir in your softened cream cheese.
  6. Grab a 8in x 8in pan and fill with dip mixture.
  7. Smooth out for even baking.
  8. Finally, bake for 20 minutes at 350, add cheese and raise temperature to 425 for the last 10 minutes (or until cheese is golden brown*).
Enjoy shortly after removing from the freezer for a cold, sweet treat!
