about me

Simple and quick recipes for everyone.

Hi, and welcome to The Bubbly Foodie, where we make simple and quick recipes. My name is Shelby, and I will be curating a list of recipes for us to try. Most of the recipes we make will have variations for those of you with food allergies/intolerances/preferences, but I will ask you to do a bit of experimenting on your own (that’s really where the fun is)! With regards to food, I am a HUGE dessert person. My friends joke that I’m trying to make them fat when I bring my dishes to our board game nights. Nevertheless, if it has carbs or sugar, I’m your lady. I look forward to cooking alongside each of you and getting to know you as we grow our community.
