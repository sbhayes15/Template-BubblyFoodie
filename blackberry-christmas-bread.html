Blackberry Cream Cheese Christmas Bread

Author: Shelby Hayes

Blackberry Cream Cheese Christmas Bread

Prep time: 5 MinsSet time: 25 MinsTotal time: 35 Mins
This is one of my favorite recipes to throw together for Christmas get togethers. So simple, and ready in 30 minutes.

Instructions

  1. Soften Cream Cheese.
  2. In a large bowl, mix cream cheese, 1 cup of powdered sugar, and 1 tsp vanilla.
  3. Set aside..
  4. Line a baking sheet with pargment paper.
  5. Place crescent rolls facing outwards, with the short ends touching and overlapping, making a base for the filling.
  6. Spread the mixture over the base of the overlapping triangles.
  7. Gently spread the jam over the cream cheese mixture evenly.
  8. Wrap the ends of the triangles over to the opposide side of the loaf, primarily covering the filling.
  9. Brush with a beaten egg.
