Frozen Greek Yogurt Bites

Yield: 6
Author: Shelby Hayes

Frozen Greek Yogurt Bites

Prep time: 10 MinCook time: 3 HoursTotal time: 3 Hrs 10 Mins
The perfect snack for the park, or even a Tuesday afternoon sweet treat for yourself! It takes 10 minutes to prep, toss in the freezer, and enjoy!

Ingredients

Instructions

  1. Mix your butter, 0.5 Tbps honey, and granola.
  2. Microwave mixture for a few seconds, to melt the butter.
  3. Grab your muffin tin and fill with cupcake liners.
  4. Fill each cup until you cannot see the bottom of the liner. Set aside.
  5. Next, mix together yogurt, vanilla, and remaining honey.
  6. Fill each cup to the top with your yogurt mixture.
  7. Decorate each cup with a few berries.
  8. Finally, pop your tray into thte freezer for 3 hours or until hardened.
Enjoy shortly after removing from the freezer for a cold, sweet treat!
